Key takeaways: What is enterprise project portfolio management (EPPM)? EPPM centralizes the management of multiple projects, aligning them with business strategy and ensuring feasibility. What are the three fundamentals of EPPM? An enterprise strategy, dedicated governance (EPMO), and powerful EPPM software are crucial for effective project portfolio management. What are the five steps to EPPM? Managers should develop their strategy by extracting goals, capturing project ideas, assessing and prioritizing projects, validating the portfolio, and monitoring progress continuously.
