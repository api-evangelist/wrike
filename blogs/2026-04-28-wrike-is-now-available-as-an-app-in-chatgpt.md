<h2><b>What this means for customers</b></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Wrike is now easier to discover, access, and adopt in ChatGPT, which is used by over 1 million business customers.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For Wrike customers, that means:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Faster setup:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Connect Wrike to ChatGPT without building custom integrations.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>More useful AI:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Give assistants access to real work context in Wrike.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Better governance:</b><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Keep permissions, access controls, and auditability in place.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><b>Less shadow AI: </b><span style="font-weight: 400;">Capture outputs in Wrike instead of losing them in chats and docs.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Instead of asking teams to switch systems, </span><b>Wrike meets them where they already work</b><span style="font-weight: 400;">. Customers can use AI assistants like ChatGPT to interact with live work data while keeping work connected to a single source of truth.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This helps accelerate both bottom-up adoption and broader enterprise rollout. Individual teams can start using Wrike with their preferred assistant, while IT and security leaders get the governance they need to support production use cases more confidently.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This availability also reinforces Wrike’s role in the evolving AI ecosystem. As organizations look for practical ways to operationalize AI, they need more than a chatbot. They need a trusted system where AI can interact with real work data securely and reliably.</span></p>

<h2><b>From AI chat to governed execution</b></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">AI can help teams move faster, but speed without control creates risk. Many organizations are already seeing the downside of </span><a href="https://www.wrike.com/ai-research-findings/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">disconnected AI usage</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">: duplicated work, siloed information, and limited visibility into what AI tools are doing.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Wrike helps solve that problem with </span><a href="https://www.wrike.com/blog/how-wrikes-mcp-server-works/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">MCP Server</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">, a secure connector that lets AI assistants read from and write to Wrike while honoring enterprise governance. That means customers can:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ask for project or task status in natural language</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Find answers across complex work structures</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Create tasks and projects from simple prompts </span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Turn meeting notes into assigned work</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Prioritize work based on deadlines and context</span></li>
</ul>
<h3><span style="font-weight: 400;">Secure by design with OAuth-based access</span></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">A key part of this announcement is that Wrike MCP Server uses OAuth-based connectivity rather than generic API keys or personal access tokens.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">For customers, this means:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Each assistant gets its own connection to Wrike</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Access can be narrowly scoped and revoked at any time</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">AI activity is more visible and auditable</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Security and compliance teams have clearer oversight</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This approach gives enterprises the flexibility to use AI assistants like ChatGPT while maintaining the controls they expect from any business-critical integration. Every action stays tied to Wrike permissions and workflows, helping organizations move faster </span><a href="https://help.wrike.com/hc/en-us/articles/34605829225746-Wrike-Model-Context-Protocol-MCP"><span style="font-weight: 400;">without compromising security</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></p>
