In 2025, we added “Engagement” as a new category to the Elite 100 to spotlight a capability high-performing teams rely on every day: meaningful participation.. At the time of these submissions, finalists used Klaxoon as a standalone visual collaboration solution to brainstorm, align stakeholders, and organize ideas more effectively. Today, Wrike supports these same types of workflows through Wrike Whiteboard, powered by Klaxoon, helping teams connect visual collaboration more directly to execution. In the era of hybrid and remote work, visual collaboration is essential for engagement.
